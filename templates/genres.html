Genre Quick-Facts

International Movies

The single largest genre tag on Netflix — reflecting its global distribution strategy beyond Hollywood.

Dramas

Consistently top-2 in both Movies and TV Shows. Prestige drama drives subscriber retention.

Comedies

Third-largest category — stand-up specials and rom-coms anchor mid-budget catalog volume.

Crime TV

Leads TV Show sub-genres — true crime and thriller series generate the highest repeat viewing.

Children & Family

Surprisingly underrepresented — Netflix's catalog skews adult, not family-oriented.

Anime

A niche but globally impactful vertical — especially powerful for audience retention in East Asia.

Genre Insights

  • International Movies is the #1 tag — Netflix's global catalog is its biggest differentiator.
  • Dramas and Comedies together account for nearly 40% of all genre tags.
  • The treemap reveals that TV Shows are more genre-concentrated than Movies.
  • Children's content is a minor category — Netflix is clearly not competing with Disney+ on family content.
