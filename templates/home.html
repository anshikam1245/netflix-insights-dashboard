Key Findings

Netflix targets mature adult audiences — TV-MA is the single most common rating across both Movies and Shows.

Content additions peaked in 2019 before COVID disrupted global production pipelines.

International Movies is the #1 genre tag — Netflix's global ambition is baked into its catalog DNA.

🇮🇳

India ranks 2nd globally in content production, reflecting Netflix's massive South Asian investment.

Over 60% of TV Shows have just 1 season — Netflix bets on limited series over long-running franchises.

Most movies run 80–120 minutes — perfectly optimised for a single streaming session.
