Country Insights

  • The USA and India together account for over half of all titled content on the platform.
  • Each country has a distinct genre fingerprint — India → Bollywood, Korea → K-Drama, Japan → Anime.
  • The sunburst chart shows the US catalog is far more genre-diverse than any other nation.
  • European countries (France, Spain, Germany) are growing contributors via Netflix's local originals strategy.
